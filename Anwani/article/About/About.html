Our Services

Architectural Services

ANWANI is a studio based practice driven by a belief that “we shape our buildings, thereafter they shape us”. The quality of our environment has a direct influence on the quality of our lives, whether at home, in the workplace or the public realm. In that sense, we view architecture and design as all embracing, ranging from the functional and environmental performance of a building to a concern for its physical, cultural and functional context. Our practice follows an interdisciplinary approach that engages urban planners, engineers, environmentalists and cost managers from the onset of each project. By working together creatively from the start of a project, the professionals combine their knowledge to devise integrated, sustainable design solutions. From appointment to completion, the design teams are supported by numerous partner disciplines, including project management and contractors. And to ensure consistency and personal service, the same core team sees a project through from beginning to end. The design process is reviewed regularly by the Design Board. Our practice follows the RIBA plan of work, 2013. This entails: • Appraisal and definition of the projects • Design concept: Design development • Approvals and technical documentation• Contract administration and inspection

Urban Planning and Design Services

ANWANI offer services ranging from concepts to the full realization of plans and their implementation. The firm has a mix of professionals with wideranging experience encompassing diverse elements of the urban and regional planning from conceptual design and feasibility studies to detailed planning and design, and contract documentation. The firm offers broad urban planning and design services, including: • Master Planning • Regional Planning Structure and Land-use Planning • Precinct and Neighborhood Planning • New Towns and Urban Extensions Planning • Land Subdivision Planning • Urban Renewal (Area regeneration and revitalization, and informal settlements upgrading) • Infrastructure Planning (Integrated Infrastructure & land-use planning; public transport facilities planning etc.) • Transit Oriented Development Planning, • Statutory Planning-Development Control and Applications for Development Approval • Consultancy in: Community planning and Development, Participatory planning, Feasibility Studies for Planning, Concept Planning and Planning Advisory services. AWNANI’s urban planning and design is informed by the commitment to promote sustainable urban development. Our approach to planning is integrated,multidisciplinary, and combines with Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and values stakeholder participation. Besides, our practice resonates with Africa’s urbanization context, by offering planning solutions that are informed by comprehensive context analysis and drawing on relevant global good practices. Our diverse urban planning and design capabilities include integration of infrastructure planning, economic productivity and financial efficiency, statutory requirements and environmental sustainability.

DEVELOPMENT PLANNING CONSULTANCY SERVICES

ANWANI has designed specialist services that aim to support clients make better informed and strategic decisions. We believe that our clients must get real value for investments.
