Architectural Services

ANWANI is a studio based practice driven by a belief that “we shape our buildings, thereafter they shape us”. The quality of our environment has a direct influence on the quality of our lives, whether at home, in the workplace or the public realm. In that sense, we view architecture and design as all embracing, ranging from the functional and environmental performance of a building to a concern for its physical, cultural and functional context. Our practice follows an interdisciplinary approach that engages urban planners, engineers, environmentalists and cost managers from the onset of each project. By working together creatively from the start of a project, the professionals combine their knowledge to devise integrated, sustainable design solutions. From appointment to completion, the design teams are supported by numerous partner disciplines, including project management and contractors. And to ensure consistency and personal service, the same core team sees a project through from beginning to end. The design process is reviewed regularly by the Design Board.


Urban Planning and Design Services

ANWANI offer services ranging from concepts to the full realization of plans and their implementation. The firm has a mix of professionals with wide- ranging experience encompassing diverse elements of the urban and regional planning from conceptual design and feasibility studies to detailed planning and design, and contract documentation. The firm offers broad urban planning and design services, including: • Master Planning • Regional Planning Federico Cortese Liveable Cities • Structure and Land-use Planning • Precinct and Neighborhood Planning • New Towns and Urban Extensions Planning • Land Subdivision Planning • Urban Renewal (Area regeneration and revitalization, and informal settlements upgrading) • Infrastructure Planning (Integrated Infrastructure & land-use planning; public transport facilities planning etc.) • Transit Oriented Development Planning, • Statutory Planning-Development Control and Applications for Development Approval • Consultancy in: Community planning and Development, Participatory planning, Feasibility Studies for Planning, Concept Planning and Planning Advisory services. AWNANI’s urban planning and design is informed by the commitment to promote sustainable urban development. Our approach to planning is integrated multidisciplinary, and combines with Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and values stakeholder participation. Besides, our practice resonates with Africa’s urbanization context, by offering planning solutions that are informed by comprehensive context analysis and drawing on relevant global good practices. Our diverse urban planning and design capabilities include integration of infrastructure planning, economic productivity and financial efficiency, statutory requirements and environmental sustainability.


Development Planning and Consultancy Services

ANWANI has designed specialist services that aim to support clients make better informed and strategic decisions. We believe that our clients must get real value for investments. This is anchored in our development planning services. We are committed to delivering cutting-edge services to the effective development of properties of different scales, from single properties to neighborhood/district level property development, for residential, commercial or mixed use property developments, as well as urban renewal projects.We believe that for investments to be effective and commercially viable, thorough analysis, strategy and vision are critical. ANWANI is committed to developing a project information resource bank for our clients. In this category, we offer services in: • Pre-Development Services: Advisory and appraisal of options; feasibility studies & site studies, urban studies and market studies, advising on statutory aspects. • Pre-Design Services: topographical and cadastral surveys; services and utilities surveys, development control requirements, environmental impact studies/assessment. • Project Management: Project Appraisal, Alternative Development Strategies, project planning.


Environmental Consultancy Services

Our value for environmental sustainability compelled ANWANI to establish a dedicated line of services on environmental consultancy. In our team we have; • Green Building Council of south Africa Accredited Professional (GBCSA-AP)-New Buildings, • EIA Lead and Associate Experts registered with the National Environment Management Authority (NEMA), and • LEED Accredited Professional with a Building Design + Construction Specialty Our specific services in environmental consultancy include: • Green Building Designs • Environmental Impact Assessment and Audits (EIA/A) • Strategic Environmental Assessment (SEA) • Organizational Health and Safety Audits (OHS) • Sustainable Urban System.


Research and Policy Consultancy Services

Research underpins our business. As a visionary firm, we strive to combine our practical work with research. We build future business through research. Research helps us to advance the potential in urban development, tap into new opportunities and sustain growth. ANWANI employs research to enhance linkages with the industry: private sector, government, academia and Non-Profit Organizations. To influence and shape a sustainable future, ANWANI goes beyond research to offer policy advice and training services to our clients. The team is committed to building reputation in research as a driver of innovative, yet practical solutions to complex human settlement issues. We offer our clients a range of research services in areas of: • Urbanization & Human Settlements • Housing and Property Development • Environment and Development Architecture and Design • Urban Planning and Design • Geographic Information Systems: Spatial Analysis & Mapping, WebGIS development • Project Based Research: baseline surveys, socio-economic surveys, participatory research, feasibility surveys and report writing. • Infrastructure design and management • Peer & Professional Reviews of Architecture, Urban Planning and Design Work • Best Practices & Case Studies
