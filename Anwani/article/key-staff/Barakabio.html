Gathua Kimani

He is the team leader and the director in charge of Architecture and Design, Environmental and Development Planning consultancy Services. Gathua has over 10 years’ experience most of which ere gained working in the design capital of Africa, Cape Town and Johannesburg, South Africa. He has also practiced in East and West Africa. He holds a Bachelor of Architecture degree from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) School of Architecture and Building Sciences (SABS) and a Master’s degree in Urban Infrastructure Design and Management from the University of Cape Town (UCT). Gathua is a Green building Council of South Africa Accredited Professional-GBCSA-AP and a NEMA certified EIA Lead Expert.

Baraka Mwau

He is the Director in charge of Urban Planning and Design, Research and Policy, and assists in managing Environmental and Development Planning consultancy Services. With more than 6 years’ work experience, Mwau has worked and undertaken projects in various countries in Africa: Kenya, South Africa, Nigeria, Malawi and Tanzania. This expertise has involved offering consultancy services to international organizations, including Shack/Slum Dwellers International and UN-Habitat; and to private sector firms and individual clients. He has published, and holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Urban and Regional Planning (First class honors) from the University of Nairobi and a Master’s degree in Urban Infrastructure Design and Management from the University of Cape Town (UCT).

Gakuhi Ngacaku

Gakuhi Ngacaku has been in the field of architecture for about 10 years. Adept at programming and assessing needs at project start-up, developing design, coordinating and documenting construction, and administering contracts, he has gained a wealth of experience working with multidisciplinary teams across Africa and North America to deliver a broad range of project types. Gakuhi holds a Bachelor of Architecture degree from the University of Nairobi, Kenya (2005). Further, as part of his continuing interest to broaden his perspective on current issues and future trends, he successfully completed a professional development program at Ryerson University, Canada (2013) comprising of ‘Construction Materials and Methods’ and ‘Fire Protection, Occupant Safety and Accessibility Design’. Gakuhi is a registered Architect is currently pursing licensure in Ontario, Canada. Gakuhi is a LEED Accredited Professional with a Building Design + Construction Specialty.
