Introduction

ANWANI is a company established in Cape Town, South Africa and later incorporated in Kenya. The company is based in Nairobi, Kenya and provides a full range of services in: Architecture, Urban Planning and Design, Development Planning, Environmental Consultancy, and Research & Policy Consultancy. We offer our services to a diverse clientele: Public Sector (government and government agencies), Private Sector (corporates/ firms and individual clients), Nonprofit/ Non-Government Organizations, and International Bilateral/Multilateral Development Organizations.


Our Focus

Our Strengths

We are a multi-disciplinary and experienced team that offers diverse and complimentary range of skills to deliver comprehensive services. For each project, ANWANI assembles a team of professionals tailored to meet the project demands, as well as the client’s requirements. Our proven track record and expertise makes ANWANI committed to providing clients with highest standard of professional services by operating as an independent consulting firm with the ethics set-down by statues governing the respective professions and as required by professional bodies.
